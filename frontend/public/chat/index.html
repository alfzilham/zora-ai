FAQ

Frequently asked questions about ZORA AI.

What is ZORA AI?
ZORA is a SuperIntelligence Autonomous AI that routes your requests to the best AI model available — Qwen for coding, Gemini for research, and more.
How does ZORA choose which model to use?
ZORA analyzes your message intent and automatically routes it to the most suitable model based on the task type.
What is Incognito Mode?
Incognito Mode lets you chat privately without saving history, profile info, or email to the session.
What is Extended Thinking?
Extended Thinking enables a deeper reasoning layer before ZORA responds, producing more accurate answers for complex queries.
What are ZORA Labs?
ZORA Labs includes specialized tools: Code, Design, Image generation, Video, and Research — each powered by the best model for that task.
Is my data safe with ZORA?
Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Incognito mode ensures no data is saved for that session.
